Mi señora,

A year ago, I didn’t know that one moment would change everything. I still see it so clearly… you standing there during Holi, red colour on your cheeks, wearing that maroon dress with your little black jacket, black Converse, and even in it those socks with a few holes. It wasn’t just how beautiful you looked… it was something about you that pulled me in without me even understanding why.

I tried to ignore it. I held back, stayed quiet, acted like it didn’t matter. But deep down, something in me already knew… you weren’t just going to be a passing moment in my life. And somehow, life brought us back to each other.

Since then, it’s been more than just love. It’s been real.

You’ve been by my side through everything, and somewhere along the way, you became my home. My peace, even in the middle of all the madness.

When I think about the future, I don’t see anything without you in it.

I see us building a life together, chasing our dreams, growing side by side… and one day, I see you as my wife. I see our family, our kids, our forever. I can’t wait for that life with you.

This past year wasn’t perfect, but it was real, and it was ours. And I wouldn’t trade it for anything.

Thank you for loving me, for staying, for choosing me even on the hard days.

This is just the beginning of us

I LOVE YOU MORE THAN WORDS CAN EVER DESCRIBE MY LOVE
